About Me

Hello! I’m Shivam Choudhary, a dedicated Safety Officer committed to ensuring safe and compliant workplaces. I specialize in risk assessments, safety training, and maintaining operational safety standards.

I have over 1 year of experience at JBS Pvt. Ltd, Tata Eureka Park, Noida, implementing safety protocols, monitoring hazards, and training teams on personal protective equipment (PPE) and emergency procedures.

I have successfully completed Fire and Safety training from NASF, Ghaziabad, equipping me with the knowledge and skills to handle emergency situations effectively.

My mission is to maintain safe, secure, and hazard-free environments while promoting a culture of workplace safety awareness.

My Goals

  • Ensure safety in the workplace every day.
  • Follow and enforce safety rules and regulations.
  • Identify hazards and reduce risks for all employees.
  • Provide training and guidance to maintain a safe environment.
  • Continuously improve safety practices and procedures.
